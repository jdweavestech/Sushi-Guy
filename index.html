HOW IT WORKS

Simple Steps to Book Your Culinary Experience

Booking your private chef or catering experience has never been easier. Whether you're planning a cozy gathering or a sushi-making session, our process ensures everything is seamless—from selecting your service to securing your spot with a quick prepayment.

Choose Your Experience

Pick the service that fits your event—be it catering, a private chef, or a fun sushi class.

Fill in Your Details

Tell us your name, email, the service type, and your preferred date and time.

Confirm with a Prepayment

Secure your booking by making a quick prepayment via Square. This holds your spot and helps us prep for your event.

Relax and Get Ready

You’ll receive a confirmation, and we’ll handle the rest. Just get ready to enjoy a delicious experience!
